About

I am a programmer working for small businesses in Brazil. My major interests are Software Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Unix, Lisp and Clojure.

I am also strongly interested in Entrepreneurship, Business Development and Management.

My full name is Rodrigo Alves Vieira and I was born in 1994, in Recife, Brazil.

I am a Computer Science student at CIn/UFPE.
