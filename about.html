About

Hi, I'm Rodrigo Alves Vieira, a 18 years old Software developer writing mostly Ruby and JavaScript code. Interested in all things Computing; primarily Web application development, Software Engineering best practices, the Unix platform, Lisp and Clojure.

I am a (freshman) Computer Science student at CIn/UFPE.

I currently work as a Software Engineer for a Brazilian tech company called Bemind, where I spend my time building critical and high-scale Web Applications.

My other interests include Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, and Web application scaling techniques.

I love open source software and, when I can, I contribute a little bit to a project, or create my own. When such things happen, my GitHub records everything.

I hang out on IRC (freenode), where my nickname is rodrigovieira. My favorite channels are #clojure, #node.js, #rubyonrails and #rails-contrib.

My personal email is rodrigovieira1994 [at] gmail [dot] com

You can follow me on Twitter and, of course, subscribe to this blog's feed.
