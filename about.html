About

THE AUTHOR

Rodrigo Vieira is a 18 years old developer, writing mostly Ruby and JavaScript code, interested in all things programming, specially Web application development and the Unix operating system.

Rodrigo is a (freshman) Computer Science undergraduate student at CIn/UFPE.

Rodrigo recently became enthusiastic with the JVM and its ecosystem, including the Java language itself, Clojure and JRuby.

His eyes sparkle also when he hears about non-relational databases and Web Application speeding and scalability techniques, although he is only a novice in all this.

Rodrigo loves open source software and, when he can, he contributes a little bit to a project, or creates his own. When such things happen, his GitHub records everything.

He hangs out on IRC (freenode), where his nickname is rodrigovieira. His beloved channels are #jruby, #clojure, #rubyonrails and #rails-contrib.

You can follow him on Twitter and, of course, subscribe to his blog's feed.

THE BLOG

This blog will publish mostly technical content on the author´s interests.

Visit the Archive to browse through all the blog posts.

All posts will be written in English. However, English is not the author´s primary language, so be friendly and send him a comment if you see a typo or any kind of grammatical error.
