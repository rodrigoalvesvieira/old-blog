About

Hi, I'm Rodrigo Vieira, a 18 years old Software developer writing mostly Ruby and JavaScript code. Interested in all things Computing; including Web application development, Software Engineering best practices, the Unix platform, the JVM, Clojure and JRuby.

I am a (freshman) Computer Science student at CIn/UFPE.

Aside with the other tools I use, I love JavaScript and, recently, Node.js.

I currently work as a Software Engineer for a Brazilian tech company called Bemind.

My other interests include fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Non relational Databases and Web application scaling techniques.

I love open source software and, when I can, I contribute a little bit to a project, or create my own. When such things happen, my GitHub records everything.

I hang out on IRC (freenode), where my nickname is rodrigovieira. My favorite channels are #jruby, #clojure, #node.js, #rubyonrails and #rails-contrib.

My personal email is rodrigovieira1994 [at] gmail [dot] com

You can follow me on Twitter and, of course, subscribe to this blog's feed.
