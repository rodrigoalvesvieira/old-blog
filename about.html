About

I am the Co-founder and CTO for Brechoh, a very young Brazilian online marketplace which is currently focusing in a segmented user base: women selling women-stuff.

I am strongly interested in Entrepreneurship, Business Development and Management.

Technically speaking, my major interests are Software Craftsmanship, Artificial Intelligence, Data Mining, Natural Language Processing, Unix, Lisp and Clojure.

I am also interested in Web Design, Information Architecture, UX and basically any field related to human-computer interaction.

My full name is Rodrigo Alves Vieira and I was born in 1994, in Recife, Brazil.

I am a Computer Science student at CIn/UFPE.

I love working for startups, being part of the conception of a business model, implementation of features, study of user feedback and everything related to the process of starting and validating a business.

I am an Open Source software enthusiast and - when I can - I contribute a little bit to a project, or create my own. My GitHub records everything.
