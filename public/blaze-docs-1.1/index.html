To get a first impression on Blaze, the short Getting Started tutorial is a good place to start. Afterwards, the following long tutorial covers all aspects of the Blaze math library. The tabs at the top of the page allow a direct access to the individual modules, namespaces, classes, and files of the Blaze library.
